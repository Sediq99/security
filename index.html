circular camera

It is a camera with a timer, where when a specific period of time passes, it rewinds around itself to be aware of the situations around it, and it is distinguished by having a very strong audio recording.


Angle camera

It is a camera that depends on being present in the corners in order to be aware of the whole place and with the highest quality.

Longitudinal camera

It is a camera that is characterized by seeing things from a very great distance and is somewhat aware of a large part of its surroundings, although it is similar to a telescope.

metal camera

It is a camera with a solid body that can be placed in places where surveillance cameras may be exposed to breakage or theft.

Rotary camera

A group of small cameras that are deployed all over the place and are of excellent quality, providing the best quality, and each camera complements the function of the other.
